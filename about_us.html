About Us

Ichiraku Ramen is the Number 1 Ramen place in Konoha Village for 50 Years due to our strong commitment to taste, giving the ramen an almost artistic quality. Ichiraku Ramen has been the favorite ramen for many past Hokage such as the Third and Fourth Hokage and remains the favorite of the current Seventh Hokage Naruto. The shop is currently run by the owner and founder Teuchi and his daughter Ayame.
